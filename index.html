A new Judge has been elected and the courtroom proceedings have changed. Now instead of a trial by jury, there is a trial by wordgame.


The Game: Hangman


You, a young lawyer, have a chance to save your client from the gallows, and return them to their life. The only way to prove their innocence is to correctly guess the word. Each wrong letter puts your client one step closer to their fate. After six wrong guesses your client's fate is sealed.
